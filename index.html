About Me

My name is Matthew Fitzgerald, and I am graduating at the end of 2024 with a B.S. in Computer Science. I have hands-on experience in developing models using machine learning techniques, as well as in automation and establishing CI/CD pipelines.

During my time in college, I completed coursework regarding programming language concepts, artificial intelligence, software development, software project management, and other relevant material. This coursework provided me with a solid foundation in both the conceptual and practical applications of computer science.

I also completed an internship focused on AWS and automation, where I gained valuable job experience. However, I am interested in machine learning, and I hope to create models that significantly exceed the performance of those used today.

Outside of my technical experience, I committed many hours to volunteer work. I really enjoyed helping the lesser-fortunate and engaging with communities outside my own. My volunteer experiences include service at food pantries, soup kitchens, homeless shelters, as well as supporting social causes. My favorite experience was volunteering at the Juneteenth 5k in Central Park, Manhattan, in 2023.

These volunteer experiences improved my social awareness and helped complement the technical one I developed by going to a technically-oriented school.
